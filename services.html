Our Services

Lightning App development

We help organizations of all sizes leverage the power of the Salesforce App Cloud platform for rapid application development. Our expertise in Lightning app development and agile approach deliver custom solutions tailored to your business needs.

Community Cloud

Community Cloud enables any business to create beautifully branded, custom communities that are completely connected to your business process, so that users of that community can effortless take action right in the community.

Sales Cloud

We have deep Sales Cloud knowledge and focuses on driving sales productivity improvement throughout the customer buying journey. We can help you transform your customer experience, grow your accounts, find new customers and close deals faster.

Service Cloud

We help your company maximize customer loyalty and profits by capitalizing on every customer interaction using the Service Cloud Salesforce Platform. We will help you increase service quality while managing costs reduction techniques such as case deflection.

Force.com Platform apps

The use of CRM applications often changes and develops over time. We have deep expertise customizing and redesigning CRM applications. We tailor a core set of proven methodologies to improve user experience, streamline process, and reduce administrative burden.

Salesforce Migration

There are many risks involved in migrating or consolidating CRM platforms. Best practices in data management and process integration are crucial. If your organization is leveraging multiple CRM systems across sales, marketing and service teams, we can help you build a centralized platform.

Integrations

Seamlessly integrate with external apps using Apex REST and SOAP services. Our salesforce development focuses on building robust APIs before focusing on designing the UI. This gives our salesforce developer to update or manipulate your data, however you want.

Reports & Dashboards

Visualize key business metrics in real-time using Lightning Experience. We create reports and dashboards that can provide the insight on where each team member or stakeholder needs to take action, whether it’s for that day, month, or year.
